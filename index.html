  • Double click on an atom deletes it
  • Double click on a bond deletes it
  • Single click on a bond changes its type
  • Single click on an atom selects it for delete; fix/ unfix; show/ hide; bonding controls in the "Atom" tab
  • Single click on 2 atoms in succession connects them with a single bond
  • Single click on the molecule viewport selects it for delete/ refresh/ recycle/ save controls in the "Molecule" tab. It also fix/unfixes it.
  • You can also manually perform atom/bond CRUD operations using the Node Id (can be fetched by hovering on the entities)
  • If the molecule is stuck after an operation, select the viewport and click on Refresh in the "Molecule" tab
